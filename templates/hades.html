#1: Hades

Hades is a fast-paced roguelike games set in the underworld of ancient greek mythology.
The main character is Zagreus, the son of hades. Zagreus has to escape the underworld
in order to reach his mother on the outside and find out the truth of his existence.
The forces of hades will try to stop him from escaping but with the help of olympic gods, Zagreus may escape.
During the game you unlock new weapons and permanent upgrades to make future attempts a lot easier
