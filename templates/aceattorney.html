#2: Phoenix Wright Ace Attorney: Trials and Tribulations

Pheonix Wright Ace Attorney: Trials and Tribulations, is the 3rd game in the Ace Attorney franchise.
The main character is Phoenix Wright, a defence attorney that has to defend his clients in court and
find out who the real culprit is. The game is divided into 2 parts per case, investigation and trial.
during the investigation you gather clues and evidence that can then be used in the trial.
trials are basically story driven logic based puzzles to find out who the real culprit is.
