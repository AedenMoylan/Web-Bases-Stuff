#3: The Legend Of Zelda: Majora's Mask

The Zelda series needs no introduction but Majora's Mask is often overlooked compared to the other games.
The main character is Link, a boy who is transported to a weird world called Termina. Link has 3 days to
stop Skull Kid from using dark powers to pull the moon closer to earth and kill everyone and everything on earth.
Link has to travel to the 4 corners of Termina to release the 4 giants that will attempt to hold the moon up.
but are the strenghts of the giants enough to support the moon?
